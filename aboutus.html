About Us

RootedTable is a web application dedicated to supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture. We are a team of passionate people who believe that every community deserves access to fresh, locally grown food.

Our mission is to connect farmers directly with consumers, making it easier for people to find and purchase locally sourced food. By creating a more direct link between farmers and consumers, we hope to support sustainable agriculture and reduce our carbon footprint.

Thank you for supporting our mission!
